Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Effective immediately

ContextMesh ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address provided during signup
  • Usage data: API query counts, entry counts, timestamps, and plan usage metrics
  • Context data: Text and metadata you store through the API
  • Payment information: Processed by Paddle — we do not store card details
  • Technical data: IP addresses, request logs, error logs for security and debugging

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Service
  • To send you your API key and account communications
  • To enforce usage limits and prevent abuse
  • To improve the Service based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your context data is stored in Qdrant vector databases hosted on Google Cloud Platform (europe-west3 — Frankfurt). Authentication data is stored in Upstash Redis. We use TLS encryption for all data in transit and encryption at rest where supported by our infrastructure providers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the following service providers necessary to operate the Service:

  • Qdrant Cloud — vector database storage
  • Upstash — Redis cache and rate limiting
  • OpenAI — text embedding generation (text is sent to OpenAI's API)
  • Paddle — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Fly.io — application hosting

5. OpenAI and Embeddings

When you store context, your text is sent to OpenAI's API to generate vector embeddings. This is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. By using ContextMesh, you acknowledge and accept this data flow.

6. Data Retention

We retain your context data for as long as your account is active. Upon account termination, data is deleted within 30 days. Usage logs are retained for 90 days for security purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Delete your data at any time via the API or dashboard
  • Export your stored context via the /list endpoint
  • Request account deletion by emailing privacy@contextmesh.dev

8. Cookies

Our landing page and dashboard use minimal cookies for session management only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact privacy@contextmesh.dev.
