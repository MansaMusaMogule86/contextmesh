Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Effective immediately

Short version: If ContextMesh doesn't work for you in the first 7 days, email us and we'll refund you. No questions asked.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your ContextMesh subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial payment. To request a refund, email billing@contextmesh.dev with your account email and reason. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.

2. Renewals

Monthly subscription renewals are generally non-refundable. If you forgot to cancel before renewal, contact us within 48 hours and we will consider a refund at our discretion.

3. How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation stops future charges immediately. You retain access to your plan until the end of the current billing period.

4. Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Accounts suspended for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Requests made more than 7 days after the charge date (unless at our discretion)
  • Partial month usage after cancellation

5. Service Credits

In the event of significant service downtime (more than 1 hour in a calendar month), we may offer service credits at our discretion. Credits are applied to your next billing cycle and are not redeemable for cash.

6. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions, contact us at billing@contextmesh.dev. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
